Participate procedure
1.Read carefully the Terms and Conditions and return the signed "Data Use Agreement Document". Students on one document are registered as a team. updated
2.We’ll get in touch with you and then reply with the data link.
3.Send us your final results by email to organizers.
Maximum number of submissions: 2 (Only the first two submissions are evaluated for a team. ) updated
Format of submissions: new
The results should be a file with name MSCMRSeg_Test_Result_<Full_Name_of_the_corresponding_author>.zip. The files in this package should be segmentation results for LGE images in *.nii.gz format. The file names for these files must contain the index of the corresponding patient. All submitted segmentation results should have exactly the same image information as the original images (including spacing, size, orientation info and short int data type etc.)
4.Submit your abstract via submission system opened.
5.Submit your paper via submission system opened following the LNCS style according to the main MICCAI conference guidelines.
The awards are evaluated based on the quality of the manuscript, presentation and test results.

Review process
For the submitted manuscripts, they will be firstly reviewed by the workshop organizers who will ensure the quality of the paper reached the publication standard. Then each paper will be reviewed by our reviewers. The review procedure will be double-blinded, similar to the MICCAI submissions. Review comments will be sent to the authors for their further consideration of modifications and preparation for the final camera ready copy that will be included in the workshop proceedings.
