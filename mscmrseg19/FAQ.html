1. Can I use semi-automatic segmentation algorithms in this challenge?
The challenge aims at both semi-automatic and fully-automatic segmentation methods. Two types of method will be ranked separately.
2. Can I publish a paper based on the validation results?
Of course you can. Please cite the following papers when you use the data for publications:
[1] Xiahai Zhuang: Multivariate mixture model for myocardial segmentation combining multi-source images. IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence (T PAMI), doi: 10.1109/TPAMI.2018.2869576.
[2] Xiahai Zhuang: Multivariate mixture model for cardiac segmentation from multi-sequence MRI. International Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted Intervention, pp.581-588, 2016.
3. Can I only use the released 5 LGE CMR as training data?
Of course. You can segment the ventricles combining the complimentary information from three-sequence CMR images, or just using the target sequence LGE CMR for training. Different types of training will be evaluated separately.
4. Can I use our own training data?
No. You’re supposed to use the data we offered for a fair comparison and for benchmarking different algorithms.
