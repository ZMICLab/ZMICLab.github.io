ZMIC

Introduction

ZMIC Lab, located in Zibin building, Fudan University, is a research group working on the feilds of medical image computing, medical imaging, and algorithm. It is led by Ph.D supervisor Xiahai Zhuang and is affiliated to the School of Data Science, Fudan University. The lab aims at joining forces to cultivate sudents' ability to analyze medical images.

There are currently students working in the group.
